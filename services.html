Full Bespoke

With this service our representative will meet with you in person or by a VOIP service such as Skype where we will discuss for at least 90 minutes what we have on offer as well as how it can meet your needs. Then we offer minimum twelve hours research based on the information that you provide and what we can find. Additional face to face or VOIP meetings can be arranged.

This is our premium service and has proved to be very popular. It is very rare that someone who uses this service and has even the minimum of information isn't fully satisfied with our work.

Bespoke

This is similar to our "Full Bespoke" service. We will still meet in person or by VOIP, this time for up to an hour. After the initial meeting further personal time can be arranged at an additional cost. For this service we offer minimum eight to ten hours intensive work on your report.

We at Western Geneological Services understand that not everyone can afford our premium service so recently we've introduced a pared down version that is affordable.

Standard

This is our most popular service for first time users. For this one of our executives will consult with you over VOIP or by telephone. Alternatively you can meet one of our contract researchers in person for up to 45 minutes. We then spend a minimum of five hours on researching and preparing a report for you.

We find that this is the minimum service that's needed to produce a report that you'll find interesting but we also find that most people who use this service like to have our researchers .

Budget

We only recently introduced a basic service in acknowledgement that some people may be reluctant to invest so much money. Our basic service does not include face to face time but we do committ to offering as much time as necessary when it comes to answering your emails.

Our basic service does not include personal time but instead we communicate by email. Since introducing this service we find that most people like to upgrade to our more costly services.

  • Ethics in business.
